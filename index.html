So what's this all about?

Celsa Arbaiza's story began when Telemundo Network began to take notice of her hard work, accomplishments, and endeavors. They featured her in a special program to share her challenging life story with the world. Since then, doors have opened to unimaginable possibilities. At the age of 24, Celsa became the CEO of her own private beauty collection by the name of “Bombastik Cosmetics”. Through professional mentorship she learned the process of properly building and marketing a business

In 2012, while in school, she took on one of the greatest opportunities that provided her financial freedom. She took on the role of developing small businesses and helping owners reach the next level through her developed network.

She established a company named Toro Taxes, whom she believed were going to become the leading and only franchise company that could tailor to the unique needs of all Latin cultures. Toro Taxes Franchising gave the exclusive rights to Celsa Arbaiza to continue the development of Toro Tax Franchises in the State of Colorado.

In addition to her current profession as a Franchise Developer for Toro Taxes, she founded a company in 2018 by the name of 1stop4you.org which is a compilation of most esteemed professional services that have never been offered to the underserved market of Latin Americans. Her company is essentially a large network of the best and greatest companies in theindustry ready to provide a high level of customer service to the forgotten minorities of this county.

Celsa's integrity is one of her main secrets of success. Celsa's ethics is her fiduciary duty, and her education is the key to open doors.

One of her priorities is to obtain her bachelor’s degree in Business, the summer of 2019, from the University of Colorado.

From 2019 foward, Celsa intends to continue her education until she becomes a law practitioner. Celsa's goal is to provide immigration and business consulting services as Attorney Celsa Arbaiza.
